Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about blood donation.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donations can be made more frequently - up to 24 times per year.

Does it hurt to donate blood?

You may feel a brief pinch when the needle is inserted, but most donors report feeling little to no pain during the donation process itself.

How long does the process take?

The entire process takes about 45-60 minutes. The actual blood donation typically takes only 8-10 minutes.

What should I do before donating?

Eat a healthy meal and drink plenty of fluids before your donation. Get a good night's sleep and avoid fatty foods for 24 hours beforehand.

Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes, blood donation is extremely safe. All equipment is sterile, used only once, and disposed of properly after each donation.
